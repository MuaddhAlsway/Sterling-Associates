# Home
The homepage serves as the primary entry point, establishing immediate credibility while guiding visitors toward key services, team information, legal insights, and consultation booking.

Layout Hierarchy:
- Header (Full-width):
  - Top Navigation Bar
- Hero Section (Full-width)
- Services Overview Section
- About Preview Section
- Blog Highlights Section
- Call-to-Action Section
- Footer (Full-width)

## Top Navigation Bar
- Law firm logo/brand
- Primary navigation links: Home, About Us, Services, Blog, Contact
- Consultation booking button (prominent)
- Mobile menu toggle (responsive)

## Hero Section
- Compelling headline emphasizing legal expertise
- Supporting subheadline describing firm specialization
- Primary call-to-action button: "Book Free Consultation"
- Secondary call-to-action button: "Explore Services"
- Professional hero image or background

## Services Overview Section
- Section heading: "Our Practice Areas"
- Section description highlighting comprehensive legal services
- Service cards (4 displayed):
  - Corporate Law card with icon, title, brief description, and "Learn More" link
  - Family Law card with icon, title, brief description, and "Learn More" link
  - Real Estate Law card with icon, title, brief description, and "Learn More" link
  - Intellectual Property card with icon, title, brief description, and "Learn More" link

## About Preview Section
- Section heading: "Why Choose Our Firm"
- Brief firm introduction highlighting experience and approach
- Key statistics or achievements (years of experience, cases won, client satisfaction)
- Team photo or office image
- "Meet Our Team" button linking to About Us page

## Blog Highlights Section
- Section heading: "Latest Legal Insights"
- Featured blog article cards (3 displayed):
  - Article 1 with thumbnail image, title, excerpt, publication date, and "Read More" link
  - Article 2 with thumbnail image, title, excerpt, publication date, and "Read More" link
  - Article 3 with thumbnail image, title, excerpt, publication date, and "Read More" link
- "View All Articles" button linking to Blog page

## Call-to-Action Section
- Strong headline encouraging consultation booking
- Supporting text emphasizing free initial consultation
- Prominent "Schedule Your Consultation" button
- Contact phone number and email

## Footer
- Firm name and brief tagline
- Quick links: Home, About Us, Services, Blog, Contact
- Contact information: phone, email, office address
- Social media links: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
- Legal links: Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions
- Copyright notice
