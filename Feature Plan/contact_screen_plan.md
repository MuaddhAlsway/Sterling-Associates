# Contact
Provide multiple convenient methods for potential clients to reach the firm and initiate consultation booking process.

Layout Hierarchy:
- Header (Full-width):
  - Top Navigation Bar
- Page Header Section
- Contact Methods Section (Two-column layout)
- Map & Location Section
- Footer (Full-width)

## Top Navigation Bar
- Law firm logo/brand
- Primary navigation links: Home, About Us, Services, Blog, Contact
- Consultation booking button (prominent)
- Mobile menu toggle (responsive)

## Page Header Section
- Page title: "Contact Us"
- Page description: encouraging visitors to reach out for legal assistance
- Breadcrumb navigation: Home > Contact

## Contact Methods Section
### Contact Form (Left Column)
- Section heading: "Send Us a Message"
- Form fields:
  - Full name input field
  - Email address input field
  - Phone number input field
  - Legal service needed dropdown selector (Corporate Law, Family Law, Real Estate Law, Intellectual Property, Other)
  - Message/inquiry textarea
  - Consent checkbox for privacy policy agreement
  - Submit button

### Contact Information (Right Column)
- Section heading: "Get In Touch"
- Office phone number with call button
- Office email address with mailto link
- Office address with full details
- Business hours information
- Alternative contact methods:
  - LinkedIn profile link
  - Twitter profile link
  - Facebook profile link

### Consultation Booking Callout
- Prominent box highlighting free initial consultation offer
- "Book Your Free Consultation" button linking to consultation booking page
- Brief description of what to expect during consultation

## Map & Location Section
- Section heading: "Visit Our Office"
- Embedded map showing office location
- Directions or nearby landmarks
- Parking information

## Footer
- Firm name and brief tagline
- Quick links: Home, About Us, Services, Blog, Contact
- Contact information: phone, email, office address
- Social media links: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook
- Legal links: Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions
- Copyright notice
